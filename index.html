Welcome to Animal Lifeline!!

Hope. Healing. A second Chance. That's what Animal Lifeline is about!

Since 1897, The Animal Lifeline-Durban & Coast associated SPCA, which is a non profit organization has been a voice for the voiceless animal in need. We are dedicated to rescuing, protecting, and caring for all types of animals in need.

We are one of South Africa's oldest and most trusted animal welfare organizations, and our mission is to prevent cruelty, promote adoption, and provide shelter and veterinary care for thousands of animals every year.

At the heart of everything we do is a deep commitment to animal welfare. We provide a safe haven for unwanted, stray, and abandoned animals, ensuring they find loving homes through our successful adoption program.

What We Do?

Our comprehensive services for animals in need

Animal Rescue

Responding to cases of neglect, abuse, or abandonment. With compassion at the heart of everything we do, our mission is to provide a safe haven for abandoned, abused, and neglected animals.

Adoptions

Finding safe and loving forever homes for animals. Through our adoption program, we connect rescued cats, dogs, and other pets with caring families ready to open their hearts and homes.

Veterinary Care

Animal Lifeline Veterinary Care provides compassionate, professional medical services to ensure the health and well-being of animals in need.

Shelter

Animal Lifeline Shelters provide a safe haven for abandoned, abused, and neglected animals, offering them love, care, and medical attention.

Education

Animal Lifeline Education aims to create awareness and understanding about animal welfare, responsible pet ownership, and compassion for all living beings.

Community Outreach

Animal Lifeline's community outreach program is dedicated to promoting compassion, responsible pet ownership, and animal welfare within local communities.
