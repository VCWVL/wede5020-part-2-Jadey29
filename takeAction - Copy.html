REPORT CRUELTY!

At the SPCA Animal Lifeline, we rely on the generosity of animal lovers and compassionate citizens to continue our mission. There are many ways you can make a real difference in the lives of vulnerable animals.

To report incidents of animal cruelty, please contact our office at 031 579 6501. For after-hours emergencies, kindly call 083 212 6103.

Alternatively, you may complete the form below, and our Inspectors will respond as soon as possible. Please do not send reports via email or Facebook, as these channels are not consistently monitored.


If the situation is urgent or life-threatening, please call our emergency number immediately at 031 579 6500. For more information, visit our official site: www.spcadbn.org.za
