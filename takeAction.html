REPORT CRUELTY!

Help us protect animals in need

At the Animal Lifeline, we rely on the generosity of animal lovers and compassionate citizens to continue our mission. There are many ways you can make a real difference in the lives of vulnerable animals.

To report incidents of animal cruelty, please contact our office at 031 579 6501. For after-hours emergencies, kindly call 083 212 6103.

Alternatively, you may complete the form below, and our Inspectors will respond as soon as possible. Please do not send reports via email or Facebook, as these channels are not consistently monitored.

If the situation is urgent or life-threatening, please call our emergency number immediately at 031 579 6500.

Volunteer Application Form

We need volunteers for a variety of tasks, including helping out at events, sorting jumble and fundraising.

I hereby acknowledge that:

  • I am 18 years of age or older. (If you are between 16-18 years of age and wish to volunteer, please email info@animallifeline.org. No volunteers under 16 are permitted.)
  • I am a voluntary worker of the Animal Lifeline.
  • I am expected to comply with the same disciplinary code as full-time employees.
  • I have read and agree to adhere to the Code of Conduct and Terms & Conditions.
  • I may be requested to accompany a staff member in an official vehicle or drive a vehicle while on duty.
  • I am responsible for my own medical expenses in the event of any injury sustained while fulfilling voluntary duties.
  • I indemnify the Animal Lifeline against all claims of any nature arising from any incident while volunteering.
  • If required to handle money, I am prepared to obtain a SAPS clearance certificate at the organization's cost.
